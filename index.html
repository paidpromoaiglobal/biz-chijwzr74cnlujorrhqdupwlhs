Our Story

Unique Fashion Clicks

Welcome to Unique Fashion Clicks, your neighbourhood destination for contemporary and stylish clothing in Kolathur, Chennai. Located conveniently near the RTO Back Side at Thirumalai Nagar Extension, Sastry Nagar, we bring you a carefully curated collection of garments that blend quality, comfort, and the latest trends — all at prices that make fashion accessible for everyone.

Since opening our doors, Unique Fashion Clicks has been committed to helping the people of Chennai dress their best for every moment — from casual everyday wear to special occasions. Our friendly staff are always on hand to guide you through our collection and help you find exactly what you're looking for.
